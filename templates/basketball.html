BASKETBALL

Hardwork beats talent when talent fails to work hard.

RULES:

  • Tournament will be played in league format.
  • During matches , If the score is same after the end of the scheduled Time ,The game shall continue with as many extra periods of 5 minutes each as is necessary to break the tie.
  • If is happens to be a league format.
  • The officiating will be done by officials who are in absolute control of the game. Teams are responsible for keeping their spectators under control. Misconduct of spectators, players or coaches can result in assessment of a technical foul, ejection or forfeiture of the game. Spectators must also remain in the area designated by the officials. The officials shall have the power to make decisions on any matters or questions not specifically covered in the rules.
  • Teams must wear shirts with the same shade of color and each shirt must have a different number
  • All players must wear non-marking rubber-soled athletic shoes
  • Jewelry of any kind (friendship bracelets, chains, rings or earrings) may not be worn. Penalty: Technical Foul.
  • Each team will have five, one minute, time outs per game with a limit of two in 1st half and 3 in second half
  • Time outs may only be called by the team with possession of the ball or during a dead ball situation
  • A player shall be allowed 5 personal fouls per game. On the fifth personal, that player shall be removed from the game. A personal fouls is a player foul that involves illegal contact with an opponent while the ball is live
  • Dunking or touching the rim during warm ups or in a dead ball situation will result in a technical foul.
  • One technical foul against a player or coach can result in immediate ejection of that person from the game. Two technical fouls constitute immediate ejection from the game and playing area.
  • Tie in the league matches will be resolved by successfully applying the following criteria :
  • If the two teams are tied head to head , the team which has won he match in the league will be places above the other team.
  • If more than two teams are equal in placing , the tie will be resolved on the basis of the ratio of “points for” to “points against “ considering the scores at the league matches between tied teams alone .
  • If the teams are still tied after the second classification , the basket average will be used to determine the placing , taking into account only the results of the games between the teams that are tied.
  • If there are still teams that are tied , the results of all their games will be used in the calculation of above .
