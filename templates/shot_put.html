RULES:

  • Once the athelete's name is called,they have 60 secons to release the shot.
  • The shot must be placed close too the neckand resting on the shoulder,while keeping it in that position the entire time until it is released.No padding or extra material is necessary.
  • The shot must be released above the height of the shoulder with one hand.
  • The athelete is permitted to touch the inside surface of the stop board; however neither the top nor the outside of the circle or stop board can be touched or passed.
  • The shot is required to land within the legal sector(34.92 degree).
  • The competitor must exit the circle from the back.
