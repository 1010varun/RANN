Success is achieved by one who is ready when the oppurtunity comes.

RULES:

  • Every team should have a maximum of 15 players and a minimum of 12 players
  • Matches will be played with daynite ball
  • Umpire decision will be final and in case of any indiscipline with umpire he may have a right to disqualify the team
  • Matches will be of 7 overs with 2 overs of powerplay
  • Team Should be in a proper dress code
  • No Player should be in more than one team, if found in more than one both teams will be Disqualified.
  • In case of a tie, Super Over will be played as per the rules of ICC
  • Overthrows will have runs but not byes and leg byes
  • No team should be involved in any indiscipline outside the game as well, if caught will be disqualified
  • Only 12 teams can register, Entry will be given as per first come first serve basis
  • Tournament will be league cum knock out
  • Teams should bring their own kits, especially Bats, none will be provided
  • Teams should look for their equipment on their own no one else will be responsible.
