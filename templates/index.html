About Rann

Presenting the fifth edition of the biggest Sports fest of Ghaziabad, RANN' 23, spanning over three days and three nights, shall be a much-needed breather from the turmoil of engineering school life. With a host of sports events to hit the ground running, it promises to be an experience that'd stick with you long after it'

  • Our Mission

    Promoting physical activity and health: Many sports events aim to encourage and inspire individuals to participate in physical activity and adopt a healthy lifestyle.!! 💫

  • Our Vision

    Providing an outstanding and memorable experience: Creating an event that participants will remember and talk about long after it is over.!!✨
